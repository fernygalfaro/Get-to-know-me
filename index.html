My Favorite Hobby

My favorite hobby is to watercool computers. Watercooling is the method of cooling a computer with water instead of air. Watercooling is achieved with radiators, fans, nickel-plated blocks, PETG or Acrylic tubes, fittings, and waterblocks. The main benefit of watercooling is to achieve better CPU and GPU clock speed while keeping system temperatures low. My favorite aspect of watercooling is the customizability to make a computer look unique. Different brands sell gold and nickel fittings, different colored coolants, and RGB fans; moreover, all these tools make a computer look colorful and aesthetically pleasing. Many people refer to customized computers as battlestations.
